# 🔒 Samplink Privacy Policy

> **Last updated:** June 23, 2026

Welcome to **Samplink** — a Chrome DevTools extension for capturing, inspecting, and mocking network requests.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Samplink collects, how it is used, and the controls available to you.

---

## ✨ At a Glance

| Topic               | Summary                                                                  |
| :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 📧 Account data     | We collect your email address only when you use authenticated features.  |
| 🌐 Network captures | Stored **only on your device** and automatically deleted after 24 hours. |
| ☁️ External uploads | Network capture data is **never sent to external servers**.              |
| 🧩 Preferences      | Mock presets stay in local browser storage.                              |
| 🤝 Data sharing     | We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or share personal data.                 |

---

## 📦 Data We Collect

### 📧 Account Data

When you create an account or sign in, Samplink collects:

* Your **email address**

Your email address is used only to:

* Identify your account
* Provide access to authenticated features

> Samplink does **not** store passwords. Authentication uses email-based flows only.

---

### 🌐 Network Request Data

While you are actively inspecting a browser tab through Chrome DevTools, Samplink may capture network request and response information, including:

* Request URLs
* Request and response headers
* HTTP status codes
* Response bodies

#### Where is this data stored?

| Property                 | Details                                            |
| :----------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- |
| 📍 Storage location      | Your browser's local `IndexedDB` storage           |
| ⏳ Retention              | Automatically deleted after **24 hours**           |
| 🚫 External transmission | Never transmitted to an external server            |
| 🎯 Scope                 | Only the tab currently being inspected in DevTools |

---

### ⚙️ Extension Preferences

Samplink stores your active mock preset using `chrome.storage.local`.

This helps restore your working state after DevTools is closed.

> This preference data remains on your device and never leaves your browser.

---

## 🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

Samplink does **not** collect:

* Browsing history
* Personally identifiable information beyond your email address
* Payment information
* Data from tabs you are not actively inspecting in DevTools

---

## 🛠️ How We Use Your Data

| Data                      | Purpose                                              |
| :------------------------ | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| 📧 Email address          | Account authentication and identification            |
| 🌐 Network request data   | Displayed within Samplink for your own use           |
| ⚙️ Active mock preference | Restores your session state after DevTools is closed |

---

## 🤝 Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

* Your email address is used only for authentication.
* Your network capture data never leaves your device.
* Your local extension preferences remain in browser storage.

---

## 🗂️ Data Retention

| Data Type               | Retention Policy                                                                 |
| :---------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 📧 Email address        | Retained for the lifetime of your account; deleted upon account deletion request |
| 🌐 Network capture data | Automatically purged from local storage after **24 hours**                       |
| ⚙️ Mock preferences     | Stored locally until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension           |

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ Your Rights

At any time, you may:

* Request deletion of your account and associated email address
* Clear locally stored extension data by uninstalling Samplink
* Clear browser storage manually through your browser settings

To request account deletion, contact us at the email address below.

---

## 🛡️ Security

Samplink is designed to keep captured network data local to your browser.

* Local data remains inside your browser's sandboxed storage.
* Account credentials are transmitted using HTTPS.
* Passwords are not stored by Samplink.
* Authentication uses email-based flows only.
